# Project Problem Pitch
----

When planning a party, guests often hvae differing diets, allergies, and restrictions that can make it hard for the party planner to choose food that everyone can eat. With our project, party planners woudl have a tool that llows them to get meal ideas that align with everyone's different dietary restrictinos, and it would also help them in their budget planning by giving a price estimate of the food.
